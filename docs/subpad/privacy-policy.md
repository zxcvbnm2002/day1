# 订阅本（SubPad）隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

## 简体中文

### 概述

订阅本（SubPad）由独立开发者提供，用于在您的设备上记录与管理订阅信息。我们重视您的隐私。本政策说明本 App 如何处理您的信息。

### 我们收集哪些信息

本 App **不要求注册账号**，也不会主动向您索取姓名、电子邮箱或电话号码。

您在 App 内输入的订阅名称、金额、扣款日期、分类、支付来源、备注及扣款记录等，均保存在**您的设备上**，包括与桌面小组件共享的 App Group 存储空间。

### 数据存储与同步

- 当前版本的数据**默认仅保存在本机**，不会上传至我们的服务器。
- 本版本**未启用 iCloud** 云端同步。
- 换机时，您可在 App **设置** 中通过 **CSV 导出** 与 **CSV 导入** 迁移数据。

### 权限与系统功能

| 功能 | 用途 |
|------|------|
| **通知** | 在扣款日前向您发送本地提醒；可在系统设置或单条订阅中关闭 |
| **面容 ID / 密码** | 若您开启 App 锁，身份验证在设备本地完成；我们不会收到生物识别数据 |
| **桌面小组件** | 读取与本 App 相同的本地订阅数据以展示摘要 |

### 第三方与追踪

- 本 App **不包含第三方广告**
- **不使用**跨 App 追踪
- **不出售**您的数据
- 未来若提供 App 内购买，交易由 **Apple** 处理，适用 [Apple 隐私政策](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

### 数据删除

- 删除单条订阅即可移除该条数据
- **卸载 App** 可从本机移除相关数据；App Group 中的数据通常也会一并清除

### 政策更新

我们可能更新本政策并在 App 内或本页面发布新版本。如有重大变更，我们将在合理范围内提醒您查阅更新后的内容。

### 联系我们

如对本政策有疑问，请发送邮件至 **[zyhp105mt@outlook.com](mailto:zyhp105mt@outlook.com)**，或通过 App Store 应用页面的「报告问题」与我们联系。

## English

### Overview

SubPad is provided by an independent developer to help you track subscriptions on your device. We care about your privacy. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

### What we collect

The App **does not require an account** and does not ask for your name, email address, or phone number.

Subscription names, amounts, billing dates, categories, payment sources, notes, and payment history you enter are stored **on your device**, including shared App Group storage used by the Home Screen widget.

### Storage and sync

- In the current version, data **stays on your device by default** and is **not uploaded** to our servers.
- **iCloud sync is not enabled** in this version.
- To move to a new device, use **CSV export** and **CSV import** in the App **Settings**.

### Permissions and system features

| Feature | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **Notifications** | Optional local reminders before billing; you can turn them off in Settings or per subscription |
| **Face ID / passcode** | If you enable app lock, authentication happens on-device; we do not receive biometric data |
| **Widget** | Reads the same on-device subscription data to show a summary |

### Third parties and tracking

- The App has **no third-party ads**
- We do **not** use cross-app tracking
- We do **not** sell your data
- If in-app purchases are offered in the future, payments are processed by **Apple** under [Apple’s Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

### Deleting your data

- Delete individual subscriptions to remove those entries
- **Uninstalling the App** removes data from your device; App Group data is generally cleared as well

### Policy updates

We may update this policy and publish the new version in the App or on this page. For material changes, we will take reasonable steps to let you know.

### Contact us

If you have questions about this policy, email **[zyhp105mt@outlook.com](mailto:zyhp105mt@outlook.com)** or use **Report a Problem** on the App Store listing.
